About Me

I'm Sukhad Sharma — a poet, a thinker, and a dreamer. I write what remains unsaid, what echoes in silences. My poems are born from midnight thoughts, quiet reflections, and raw emotions. This blog is a digital diary, where every verse holds a piece of me.

I don’t follow rules. My poems break structures, ignore rhymes when they must, and let emotions lead. If you're looking for something real, unpolished, and honest — you’ll find it here.
